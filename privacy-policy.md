# Privacy Policy – solXbus

Last updated: January 2026

## Overview
solXbus is a local Modbus TCP diagnostic tool for industrial devices.

The app does **not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data**.

## Data Collection
- No personal data is collected
- No analytics or tracking services are used
- No third-party services are integrated

## Network Usage
All network communication happens **locally within the user’s own network** (e.g. Modbus TCP devices).
No data is sent to external servers.

## User Data
solXbus does not create user accounts and does not store any user-identifiable information.
